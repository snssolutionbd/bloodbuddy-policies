Delete Your BloodBuddy Account

You can permanently delete your BloodBuddy account.

How to delete

  1. Open BloodBuddy.
  2. Login.
  3. Go to Profile.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm deletion.

Data Deleted

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone Number
  • Blood Group
  • Profile Information

Data Retention

Any information required by applicable law may be retained only for the legally required period. All other personal data is permanently deleted.
