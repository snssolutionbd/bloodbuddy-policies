1. Introduction

BloodBuddy ("we", "our", "us") is developed and maintained by SNS Solution BD. BloodBuddy is a community platform that connects people from the same study batches (SSC, HSC, University), facilitates blood donation, community meetups, job postings, and a community marketplace.

By using BloodBuddy you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue using the application.
