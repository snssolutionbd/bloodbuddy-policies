Zero Tolerance Policy

BloodBuddy has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse, child sexual exploitation, grooming, trafficking, or any content or behavior that harms minors.

1. Our Commitment

SNS Solution BD is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and trusted community. We prohibit any content, behavior, or activity that exploits, sexualizes, abuses, or endangers children or minors.

2. Age Requirements

  • BloodBuddy is intended for users aged 13 years or older.
  • Users under 18 should use the platform with parental or guardian permission where required by applicable law.
  • We do not knowingly allow children under 13 to register.
  • If we determine that an account belongs to a child under 13, we will remove the account and associated personal information as appropriate.

3. Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming behavior
  • Soliciting personal information from minors for harmful purposes
  • Sharing or requesting exploitative material involving minors
  • Harassment, bullying, or threats directed at minors
  • Arranging harmful meetings with minors
  • Human trafficking or exploitation involving minors

4. How We Protect Children

  • Registered-user community platform
  • Community-based access
  • User reporting tools
  • Content moderation and policy enforcement
  • Removal of violating content
  • Account suspension or permanent bans for confirmed violations

5. Reporting Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter content or behavior that may violate this policy, contact us immediately.

Email: snssolutionbd@gmail.com

Subject: Child Safety Report — BloodBuddy

Please include:

  • Description of the incident
  • Username or profile link (if available)
  • Screenshots or supporting evidence (if safe to provide)
  • Date and time of the incident

6. Our Response

When we receive a credible report, we may:

  • Review the reported content or account.
  • Remove content that violates our policies.
  • Suspend or permanently terminate violating accounts.
  • Preserve relevant evidence where appropriate.
  • Cooperate with law enforcement authorities when required by applicable law.

7. External Reporting Resources

  • NCMEC CyberTipline
  • Internet Watch Foundation
  • Bangladesh Telecommunication Regulatory Commission (BTRC)

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in applicable laws, Google Play requirements, and our child safety practices.
