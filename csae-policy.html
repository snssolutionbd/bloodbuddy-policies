Child Safety Standards

BloodBuddy has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Commitment

  • We prohibit child sexual abuse and exploitation.
  • We prohibit grooming or exploitation of minors.
  • We prohibit sharing illegal or abusive content.
  • Violating accounts will be suspended or permanently removed.
  • We cooperate with law enforcement where legally required.

Reporting

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these standards, please contact:

support@bloodbuddy.com

Review Process

Reports are reviewed promptly. Appropriate action, including account suspension or removal, will be taken where necessary.
